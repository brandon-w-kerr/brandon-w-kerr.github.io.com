SUMMARY

Hello! My name is Brandon Kerr. I'm a 17-year-old boy who was born in New-York, and moved to North Carolina. Technology has played a big role in my life, which is why I love it so much and want to be a Computer Engineer! Before I had this interest, I actually wanted to be an Animator or a Chef because Ratatouille is my favorite movie of all time but I didn't know how to cook (still don't unfortunately...) and I used to be very hard on myself while I was drawing. After I was done drawing I would use the computer to play video games, which mainly consisted of Sonic The Hedgehog because it is my favorite series (still is! I know almost everything about it!) Playing video games on the computer was an unforgettable experience but I remember questioning to myself "how DOES a computer work?" which prompted the snowball to start moving.
